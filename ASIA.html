INTRODUCTION

Asia is the largest among all continents, recognised for its wide range of cultures. It Contains 48 countries, and has a population of 4.56 crores,
more than half of the whole earths population! Asia boasts unparalleled cultural diversity, featuring a rich tapestry of languages, religions,
and ethnicities. From the towering peaks of the Himalayas to the vibrant megacities of Tokyo and Mumbai, Asia's geography is as
diverse as its people. As a global economic powerhouse, Asia's influence extends across technological innovation, economic growth,
and geopolitical dynamics.
